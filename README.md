## Project-Idea

### 1.1 Summary
CarGuy is a platform for everyone, who loves cars, has or wants a car. People can post pictures of their cars, ask questions, in general or about specific models, and post their opinions. It will work like a big forum, with featured guides, posts and communities one can join. Our goal is to have one platform for all the social needs a "car guy" may have.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

People owning cars have to look through dozens of different forums to have their questions answered, they may not even find solutions to a problem they are having.

---

## 3. Objectives

- Enable social interaction between users
- Provide high-quality guides in one central place
- Make car-related information and opinions easy to find


---

## 4. Scope
### 4.1 In Scope

- User accounts and profiles
- Publishing posts and images
- Featured guides
- Filtering by car brand and model

### 4.2 Out of Scope

- AI assistant
- A built in car recommendation tool
- Being able to purchase cars
